What is Warcraft III?

Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne is a real-time strategy computer game by Blizzard Entertainment.It supports both PVE and PVE. It includes four tribes, twenty-four heroes including 8 neutrals, and all kinds of interesting fighting units.

Once known as one of the most prevalent game, it owns vivid background stories, which also charms a great number of players and fans.

About Tribes
ORC HUM NE UD
Orc is a aggressive tribe. It has four heroes, BladeMaster, FarSeer, TaurenChieftain and ShadowHunter. They once lived in another world called Draenor， their warchief, Gul'dan, induced by Sargeras，invaded Azeroth，together with Blackhand. Human is a hard-working tribes. It has four heros: Archmage， MountKing, Paladine and BloodMage, who used to be a branch of NE elite.It is also known for its building (tower) civilization and advanced mechanical weapons like tank and helicopter. Ne is one of the eldest indigenous tribe. Once it was a feminist country famous for its magic. But after a great disaster, they raised some strict hold on magic use. Its typical heroes are: DemonHunter, Warden, Priest Of Moon and Keeper Of Grove. UD was created by the Burning legion but the Lich King betrayed the legion and get independence. It's known for its fantastic ability of tranforming the dead to its unit.The weapon Frostmourne could revive Paladines and Archmage into DeathKnight and Lich.
